[Rule]

When all the grids other than a mine are opened, it is the game clear!
It is game over when a mine is opened.

[About the grid of a number]

It is meant how many mines an adjoining grid has.
For example, in the case of 2, it means that the surroundings have two mines as shown in the figure.

[Let's stand a flag to a place with a mine]

As shown in the figure, when the position of a mine can be pinpointed, let's stand a flag and make it not open a grid.

About The Game Screen

  • main area
  • controller
  • header

[Main Game Area]

- Tap to open a square other than mines.

- Long tap to put/remove the flag.

- In hint mode, safe squares are highlighted. (Image red frame)

[Controller Bar]

You can switch modes by tapping each button.

(1) Mode to open squares. Normally operate in this state.

(2) Mode in which only flags can be placed. It is used when you want to put in multiple places continuously.

(3) You can enlarge or reduce the game area. It is used for grasping the board surface.

(4) Tap to display one hint. A cooldown of 30 seconds will occur when you tap once.
* Squares highlighted with hints can be opened safely.
* Please use it effectively when you do not understand.

[Header display]

You can check the status display, etc.

(1) Score, number of squares, total number of bombs.

(2) Timer
* Countdown will be performed in ranking mode.

(3) Interrupt button. A game-pause modal opens.
